Scientific Journal


Scientific Journal of the Faculties of Commerce Sector :

The first issue of the magazine started in December 1978 until issue thirty-one for the year 2006. The issue was issued under the name of the Scientific Journal of Al-Azhar Commerce.
After that, the name of the magazine was changed to the Scientific Journal of the Faculties of Commerce Sector at Al-Azhar University . The first issue of the sector was issued in 2007. Then the second issue of the sector was issued in 2008.
