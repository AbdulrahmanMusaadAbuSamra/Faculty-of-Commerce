Speech of His Excellency the Dean of the College

Professor Dr. Adel Mamdouh Gharib
In the name of God, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful

Praise be to God, Lord of the Worlds, and prayers and peace be upon the most honorable of prophets and messengers, our master Muhammad, and upon all his family and companions, and upon those who follow his guidance until the Day of Judgment.

The Faculty of Commerce for Boys is one of the faculties of Al-Azhar University, which contributes to achieving the university's mission in education, scientific research and community service through the academic programs it offers in various specializations at the undergraduate level as well as at the academic and professional postgraduate levels in administrative and financial sciences and other related sciences, as well as the training programs offered by the Training and Consulting Center. This brief guide is intended to introduce the faculty and the academic, professional and training programs it offers at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels.

We ask God Almighty that this site contributes to providing students, researchers and those interested with important services and information about the college and its contribution to achieving the university’s mission in education, learning, scientific research and community service. Dean of the College Prof. Dr. Adel Mamdouh Gharib
